Vibe-code from your phone. Your Claude. Your tailnet. No relay.

Tiny Node bridge on your laptop + Expo app on your phone, peer-to-peer over your own Tailscale. Swipe left in the chat → live WebView of the dev server your agent is editing. Edit a CSS variable from your phone, watch the page re-theme on the same screen.

MIT, no accounts, no telemetry: https://github.com/AleksandreJavakhishvili/Rove
